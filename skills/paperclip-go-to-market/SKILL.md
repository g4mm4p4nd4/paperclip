# Paperclip Go To Market

Use this skill when the question is how to get the right people to care, act, and come back.

## When to Use

- launch planning
- messaging and positioning
- ICP and wedge definition
- growth experiments and distribution
- product marketing and content sequencing

## Workflow

1. Define the target user and their urgent problem.
2. State the product promise in plain language.
3. Choose the channel and the call to action.
4. Build the smallest launch plan that can generate signal.
5. Track what will be measured before the work ships.

## Rules

- Do not invent proof that the product does not have.
- Match the message to current product reality.
- Prefer a narrow believable claim over a broad vague one.
- Tie growth tasks to concrete metrics and review windows.

## Output Expectations

Good output from this skill should include:

- audience
- message
- offer or CTA
- channel plan
- launch assets needed
- measurement loop
